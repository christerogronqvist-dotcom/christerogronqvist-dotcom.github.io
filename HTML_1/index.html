Dune

Directed by: Denis Villeneuve

Genre: Epic Science Fiction

Year Released: 2021

Major Awards: Winner of 6 Academy Awards
Fun Fact: The ornithopter design was inspired by dragonflies.

Dune tells the story of Paul Atreides, a brilliant young man born into a great destiny.
He must travel to the most dangerous planet in the universe to ensure the future of his family and his people. As malevolent forces explode into conflict over the planet's exclusive supply of the most precious resource in existence, only those who can conquer their fear will survive. The film is a stunning adaptation of Frank Herbert's novel, praised for its immersive world-building.
